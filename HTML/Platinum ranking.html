What is a Platinum?

To talk about a platinum, we first have to introduce Playstation's Trophy system. Playstion added a trophy system to reward gamers for achieving certain tasks in games.These trophies all combine into a playstation level, which is one measurement to show how accomplished of a gamer you are. The are 4 types of trophies:


Bronze:

Awarded for easy tasks that needs 10 secs-1 hour and barely provide any progress to a level.

Silver:

More difficult taks that take anywhere from 30 mins-10 hours to achieve. They give some progress towards a level.

Gold:

Extremely difficult tasks that take the average person 5-20 hours to complete. Provide a huge amount of progress towards a level, and at lower level could complete a level on their own.

Platinum:

To get a platinum trophy is a game, you must do EVERYTHING that you can possibly do in that game. dpending on the game it takes 15-60 hours, but in extreme cases, it could take up to 100+ hours.

8. Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons is a great game, but the extra content that the game has you do to get the platinum is extremely repetitive. You have to keep on playing the same levels until you get rare items. If the trophies just had you finish the main game instead of collecting each item, it would easily rank a lot higher.

7. Cult of the Lamb

Cult of the Lamb is one of the best games I have played, but the Platinum requires you to get a lot of rare skins and items. This alone wouldn't be that much of a problem, but the game being riddled with glitches as you progress in the game makes it a lot harder and prevents you from fully utilizing mechanics of the game, which make it easier to acquire those skins.

6.Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart

This game is boring overall. It is way too easy, even on the hardest difficulty and the combat system is very boring. All you do is shoot enemies and then wait until your ammo refills, so it is basically just waiting. The game didn't take much time to complete and the Platinum afterword was easy, but since I didn't have to waste to much time to repetitiveness or glitches it ranks 6th.

5.Spider-Man: Miles Morales

Spider-man is a great game with and entertaining combat system and relatively simple tasks to do for the Platinum. None of the tasks make me go out of my way to get. The game is very smooth with very advanced graphics.

4. Spider-Man 2

This is a great game with challenging combat. The Platinum is very similar to the Spider-Man: Miles Morales. The that it ranks higher is that it's combat is more fun, with some bosses taking up to one hour to defeat and the fact that you get to play with both of the Spidermen, which have new abilities makes the game a blast to play through.

3. Minecraft

Minecraft itself is probably the best game to have ever existed. The Platinum requires you to do some obsecure achievments, but none of them are boring or too hard. Most players will go through hundreds of hours without getting the Platinum because it needs some trophies that are out of the way.

2. Astro's Playroom

This game is the Playstation 5 tutorial game. Even though it is a very short experience it is still magical. The combat is easy, but the real appeal of this game is the puzzles, easter eggs, collectables, and creativity. Every section of the game is unique. Just to show how good this game is, they made an extended version of it called Astro Bot, which won Game of the Year for 2024. Another aspect that makes getting the Platinum so fun is that you enver have to search for how to acquire an achievement since the game has a built in system that shows you how to get those trophies.

1. Ghost of Tsushima

This game was my favorite to Platinum. There weren't any usless trophies that I had to go for. All of them made me stronger as I played, and allowed me to experiment with new playstyles. The games combat at the beggining was very hard, fair, and fun, but this is the only game in this list that I didn't play on the hardest difficulty. I wish I did because even though the combat was still fun later in the game, I think it would've been even more fun if it was harder. The combat in very unique and I can't wait for the sequel.
